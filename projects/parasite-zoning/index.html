The Financial District, Midtown, and Upper West Side, in Manhattan, lack much diversity in functions of the buildings and the land use pattern.

Our proposition is a new system of urban inserts. In the parasite zoning system, the original zoning system that Manhattan has used for decades remains and parasite capsules are attached to the building, which forms new zoning in the air vertically.
