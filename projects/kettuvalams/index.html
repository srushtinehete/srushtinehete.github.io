The competitions aimed at discovering and preserving local and traditional craft forms which are involved with building techniques for habitation. It focuses on conservation by means of thorough documentation of the unknown crafts and craftsmen.

KETTUVALAMS

We took up the art of making ‘kettuvalams’, traditional boat making in Kerala. Craftsmen that make houseboats in the traditional way, using local materials and techniques. The deliverables were analysis, documentation and presentation in form of hand done sheets.

This trophy aims to provide an opportunity to students of architecture, for explorations in a lesser known but highly significant wealth of knowledge and wisdom embodied in the construction traditions of India and its regions. To encourage them to develop a deeper and more holistic understanding of processes and human skills connected with traditional construction in all regions of the country.
